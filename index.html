Bitcoin Blockchain Explorer

To view a block on the Bitcoin blockchain, enter an integer from 0 to around 700,000 for best results.

Note: If you enter a large number, the information may load slowly. Please be patient.

Some example blocks to try:

  • 0 - The Genesis block (the first block on the blockchain)
  • 170 - The first block on the blockchain with a non-coinbase transaction.
  • 700,000 - This block has a lot of transactions.
